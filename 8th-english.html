Story Explanation

Tenses

Present, Past, Future - Simple, Continuous, Perfect, Perfect Continuous. Example: I eat (Present), I ate (Past), I will eat (Future).

Active & Passive Voice

Active: Subject + Verb + Object (Ram eats an apple)
Passive: Object + Helping Verb + V3 + by + Subject (An apple is eaten by Ram)

Direct & Indirect Speech

Direct: He said, "I am happy."
Indirect: He said that he was happy. (Reporting verb, tense, pronouns change)

Parts of Speech

Noun (नाम), Pronoun (सर्वनाम), Verb (क्रिया), Adjective (विशेषण), Adverb (क्रियाविशेषण), Preposition (संबंधसूचक), Conjunction (समुच्चयबोधक), Interjection (विस्मयादिबोधक)

Essay & Letter Writing

Formal Letter: To officials, Principal; Informal Letter: To friends, family; Essay on important topics like Pollution, My School, Importance of Education

Synonyms & Antonyms

Synonyms (समानार्थी): Happy → Joyful, Glad, Cheerful
Antonyms (विलोम): Happy → Sad, Unhappy, Miserable
